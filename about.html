Our Story

DeepLearning.Al was founded in 2017 by machine learning and education pioneer Andrew Ng to fill a need for world-class A1 education.

DeepLearning.Al has created high-quality A1 programs on Coursera that have gained an extensive global following. By providing a platform for education and fostering a tight-knit community, DeepLearning.Al has become the pathway for anyone looking to build an A1 career.
